CROSS ENROLLMENT PROCEDURE

Malayan Colleges Mindanao is now accepting students from other colleges and universities to cross enroll their fully online courses starting 1st Semester SY: 2020-2021.

Step 1

Apply through this link: https://bit.ly/CrossEnrollmentRegistration

Step 2

Confirmation of course availability and letter of acceptance will be sent through the email address provided during registration

Step 3

Email a scanned copy or a clear photo of the following enrollment requirements to admissions@mcm.edu.ph. Please make sure to fill out the forms completely and sign the document.

-Application Form (Download here: https://mcm.edu.ph/downloadables/)

-Endorsement Letter from the student’s school of origin.

Step 4:

Wait for an email from the Registrar’s Office with the generated schedule, assessment, and payment procedure.
